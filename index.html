🎯 Purpose of this System

This system serves as a comprehensive digital guide for field engineers and technicians involved in substation commissioning and maintenance. It provides step-by-step testing procedures, automated fault analysis, safety interlocks, troubleshooting logic, and an AI-powered chat assistant — all in one place.

📦 Equipment Coverage
⚡ Circuit Breakers (SF₆, Vacuum, Air-Blast)
🔁 Power & Distribution Transformers
📡 Current & Potential Transformers
🔋 Station Batteries (VRLA, NiCd, Lead-Acid)
🛡️ Safety Checks & Interlock Verification
📊 Automated Fault Report Generation
